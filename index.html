HIGH TECH INFRASTRUCTURE PLAN

About

The Innovation Team (i-team), the Technology & Innovation Department (TID), and Public Works are in the process of developing a "master plan" to invest in the City's high tech infrastructure to promote economic, social, civic, and workforce development.

Strategic use and maximization of City assets will result in increased investment and quality internet access for businesses and residents.

Displayed below is a visual map of the City's current fiber optic assets, proposed expansions, and local Internet Service Providers (coming soon).
